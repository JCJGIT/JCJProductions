Q - What is SEND?
A – SEND stands for Special Educational Needs and Disabilities, SEND is the category you come under if you have struggles at school which are due to mental disabilities or if you are disabled. Schools are legally required to give you aid to give you extra support to be able to have better education but also to support you for later on in life where you may struggle.

The Game

The game I am creating is going to be called Spaceflight: Asteroid Escape, In this game you as the player are going to be controlling a ship and as the title suggests, the core principles of the game is to escape the asteroid fields, This game is going to be a make it as far possible game like Crossy Road and Pac-Man 365 both featuring on the mobile app stores. You as the player have to manoeuvre through asteroid fields to avoid being hit and being destroyed, by doing this you will want to aim to get the highest scores possible.

As you venture through the asteroid fields you will encounter power ups and coins, The power ups will allow you to get a timed advantage in the format of taking no damage by protection of a shield or a more powerful laser beam compared to the basic one shot weapon already equipped to the ship. The coins will allow you as the player to be able to upgrade aspects of the game to allow you to go further like for example upgrade the shield power up to last longer or go faster or make the laser become more powerful, essentially your efforts will be rewarded which will then allow you to venture further.

Of course you cannot have a space game without the involvement of some form of alien lifeform so featuring in my game I intend to have a boss wave to really test your abilities, it would get boring just dodging asteroids constantly so we want to test you skills in which you will have to tackle an alien out for vengeance this will be triggered after you make it a certain distance.
