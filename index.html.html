THE GAME

The Game

For the commission I have received from SEND I have to create a game which will be played by kids 10 – 16. The game must be colourful, educational and engaging for the player to feel engaged and interested.

The game that I am making is called Space Flight: Asteroid Escape, the purpose of this game is to make it as far as possible without being destroyed by various dangers including (Asteroids and Enemies). You will encounter a varied environment as you progress through the levels where the asteroid levels vary depending on how far you make it, also you will gain power ups which will help you get an advantage and make it further then you might without, the two power ups you can find is a laser core and shield core, the laser core will allow you to make a new path by destroying asteroids and enemies whilst the shield core will assist in making you immortal to damage for 15 seconds, these power ups can be upgraded by gaining coins as you go which can increase their abilities to make things more easy.
